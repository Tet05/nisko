Nisko Technologies Ltd. (formerly IES Electronics Agencies) is a subsidiary of the Nisko Industries group, acting as a leading distribution and representation company within the Israeli market. On top of the wide products range from market leading companies, being our esteemed vendors, Nisko Technologies is offering a selection of in-house capabilities which allows the optimal match of products and solutions being offered to our customer needs. Those products are being offered within the following 2 groups:

Embedded & Vision solutions Test & Measurement equipment

Each group is an independent business unit that uses cross-organizational solutions in order to provide a one-stop-shop concept. The synergy between the Nisko Technologies groups and the cooperation within the Nisko organization companies enables us to offer our customers a wide range of products and innovative solutions, standard or customized. Technical support is the underlying factor in our operation and a source of great pride to Nisko Technologies. Since its establishment, more than 30 years ago, Nisko Technologies has adapted a unique solution-driven approach. We offer our services from the very early consulting stage, through the design phase and up to full project deployment.

Our unique and modern infrastructure ensures the provision of efficient, professional, competitive and profitable services from which our customers and principals seek benefit.

Each of our product groups enjoys the first class services, provided centrally, such as the professional management, fully computerized order processing and order management system, import/export services, stock management, Marcom, and others.

Our constant participation in relevant local trade shows, seminars and conferences, our comprehensive advertising in local magazines and our large quality database of customers, ensures optimal exposure of our principals and their technologies within the market.
